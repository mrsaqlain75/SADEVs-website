How to earn on SADEVz.com? Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1
First of all, you have to sign up to SADEVz.com by clicking on the Sign Up button at the top right.
Step 2
Next, you have to login to SADEVz.com.
Step 3
After logging in, click on the Profile icon at the top right and select Edit Profile and provide the necessary information.
Step 4
After completing your profile, click on profile icon and click on Post Article.
Step 5
Here, you will see a text editor. You have to give an appropriate and short title to the article and then write your content inside the text editor. You can upload pictures if you want; by clicking the file upload button at the buttom and you can also style your content; as you do in MicroSoft Word.
Step 6
After writing the article, revise your article before submitting it, as you won't be able to modify it before 1 month. (This issue will be cleared in a month, as the website is under development)
Step 7
After submitting the article, your article will be up for approval which can take time between 1 hour to 2 days.
Step 8
After having three posts on SADEVz.com, you will get a notification within a month for partnership, in which you would write for SADEVz.com and based on the quality of your content and user's review, you will be paid.
Step 9
Remember, SADEVz.com only needs original content. AI generated content will lead to permanent blockage of your account from SADEVz.com.
So, let's write and earn!
